## üìä POSITIONING VALIDATION CHECKLIST

Test your positioning statement:

### **Clarity** (Can a 12-year-old understand it?)
- [ ] No jargon or buzzwords
- [ ] Concrete words, not abstract
- [ ] Specific, not vague

### **Differentiation** (Is it obviously different?)
- [ ] Clear "unlike X, we Y" statement
- [ ] Not just "better" but "different"
- [ ] Competitors can't claim the same thing

### **Relevance** (Does target audience care?)
- [ ] Addresses real pain point
- [ ] Outcome that matters to them
- [ ] Speaks to their priorities

### **Credibility** (Can you prove it?)
- [ ] Have customer evidence
- [ ] Can demonstrate it works
- [ ] Not aspirational, actual

### **Memorability** (Will they remember it?)
- [ ] Short enough to repeat
- [ ] Concrete imagery
- [ ] Emotional resonance

**Score: ___/15**

If score < 12, revise your positioning!
